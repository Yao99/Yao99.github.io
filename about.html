About Me!

I'm a senior at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Game Development with a concentration in Human-Computer Interaction. I want to create games that are enjoyable for everyone, and what better combination can accomplish that than game design and HCI? From my first level that I created in the Super Smash Bros. Brawl level editor, I fell in love with creating game content that can be enjoyed by all. It brings a smile to my face seeing people have fun in something I helped create.

I've explored Virtual Reality game development, worked with interactive AI, created platformers, mobile games, and have ample experience in both Unity and Unreal Engine 4. As of right now, I am seeking a Summer 2020 internship that can challenge me to think outside the box and be part of something greater.
